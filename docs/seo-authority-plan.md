# SEO Authority Plan — SA Learn

The authority programme must be earned through useful, source-backed public assets. It must not rely on purchased links, fabricated testimonials, mass-generated pages or unverified education claims.

## Cornerstone assets

1. Public institution directory covering South Africa's universities and TVET colleges, with official source links and verification dates.
2. Plain-language APS, NQF, SAQA, NSFAS, accreditation and application guides.
3. Application-deadline and opportunity pages backed by official sources and expiry controls.
4. Transparent study-path matching methodology, including its limitations.
5. Province and institution-type comparison pages only when the underlying data is complete enough to support them.
6. Downloadable school counsellor and parent guidance resources.

## Partnership targets

- Public universities and TVET colleges
- School counsellors and educator networks
- Youth-development NGOs
- Bursary and learnership providers
- Libraries and community learning centres
- Provincial and municipal youth programmes
- Reputable education publishers and journalists

## Outreach principles

- Lead with a specific useful asset, correction channel or data partnership.
- Ask institutions to verify their profile and link to the canonical public page.
- Offer embeddable, attributed resources only when updates can be maintained.
- Record relationship owner, contact date, response and next action outside public learner data.
- Never exchange money, hidden promotion or favourable ranking for editorial links.

## Measurement

Track monthly:

- referring domains;
- links to cornerstone guides and institution pages;
- verified institution profile participation;
- non-branded organic landing pages;
- search impressions and clicks;
- corrections received through official partners;
- expired or withdrawn content.

## Initial sequence

1. Complete source and attribution review for all public institution profiles.
2. Publish and validate the core APS/NQF/NSFAS/accreditation guides.
3. Prepare a concise institution verification request.
4. Contact a small, representative pilot group.
5. Improve assets from their corrections before wider outreach.
